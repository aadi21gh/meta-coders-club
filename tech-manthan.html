Tech Manthan 2025

Where ideas collide and innovation thrives.

Poster & Promotion

Tech Manthan is the annual flagship event of Meta Coders Club that brings coders, innovators, and tech enthusiasts together to showcase their creativity.

Date: 18 – 20 October 2025 | Venue: Main Auditorium, IIT Madras

Event Details

Tech Manthan 2025 featured coding battles, AI challenges, and project showcases. Participants built innovative solutions for real-world problems, competing for top prizes.

Event Outcome

The event concluded with over 120 participants, 20+ projects, and outstanding ideas in AI, Web, and IoT. Team “CodeX” won the grand prize for their AI-powered traffic optimization system.

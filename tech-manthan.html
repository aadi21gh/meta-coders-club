Event Details

Tech Manthan was an exciting and intellectually stimulating event organized by the Meta Coders Club to challenge the logical, analytical, and coding skills of participants. The event was designed to ignite curiosity, teamwork, and technical problem-solving among students through two thrilling rounds: Scan & Seek and Code Cascade.
